Find a Shelter

An animal shelter or pound is a place that stray, lost, abandoned or surrendered animals, mostly dogs and cats and sometimes sick or wounded wildlife, are kept and rehabilitated. While no-kill shelters exist, it is sometimes policy to euthanize animals that are not claimed quickly enough by a previous or new owner.

Our goal is to stop the euthanization of these loving animals and give them homes in loving places. Please consider donating and/or adopting one of these precious animals.

Adopting a Pet

We recommend visiting the ASPCA website to see if there's any shelters near where you live.

Remember, you can always adopt a dog as well! As long as these loving animals get loving homes.
